Short-bio

I am a graduate student in Nanjing University, and I am expected to graduate in July 2020.

Now I am working in Urban and Thermal Remote Sensing Group in Nanjing University, under the instruction of Prof. Wenfeng Zhan. My recent published papers focused on the uncertainties in the satellite-based quantification of the Surface Urban Heat Island (SUHI), as well as the typical diurnal patterns of the SUHI intensity. For more details, please see Research and Publication.

My research interests include: urban climate, remote sensing, urbanization, and land use and land cover change.
