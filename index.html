Short-bio

Jiameng Lai is a Ph.D. candidate in Cornell University, and she is expected to graduate in July 2025.

Now She is working in terrestrial carbon-water cycle using biosphere model and satellite remote sensing, under the instruction of Prof. Ying Sun. Before joining Cornell, her research focused on urban climate and thermal remote sensing, instructed by Prof. Wenfeng Zhan. For more details, please see Research and Publication.

Her research interests include: global water-carbon cycle, terrestrial biosphere model, ecology, remote sensing, urbanization, urban climate.
